How Harbor Works

1

Connect Through Personality

Discover someone through their thoughtful responses to meaningful prompts. No photos upfront—just authentic personality and genuine connection.

2

One Connection at a Time

Focus on one meaningful conversation at a time. This creates space for genuine connection and authentic interaction without distractions.

3

Gradual Photo Reveal

After building a genuine connection through conversation, you'll both decide together when you're ready to see each other. Mutual respect and consent guide every step.
