Cornell Dating Relationship App

Form meaningful connections without the superficiality of typical dating apps. Not about pick-up lines or polished selfies. Just real relationships.

How Harbor Works

1

Swipe on Personality

See responses to six thoughtful, short-answer prompts. No profile photos upfront—just personality.

2

One Match at a Time

You can only talk to one person at a time. This ensures your attention is undivided and intentional.

3

Gradual Photo Reveal

After 20–30 minutes of chatting, your matches gradually becomes clear. You both must agree to continue before seeing the image.

Why Harbor?

Personality & Attraction
