Support

Last Updated: June 29, 2025

Need help with Harbor? We're here to assist you with any questions, technical issues, or concerns you may have.

Contact Us

For support, questions, or feedback, please contact us at:

  • tryharborapp@gmail.com

What We Can Help With

  • Technical issues with the app
  • Account-related questions
  • Privacy and security concerns
  • Billing and subscription issues
  • Feature requests and feedback
  • General questions about Harbor

Response Time

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days.

Before Contacting Us

To help us assist you more efficiently, please include:

  • A clear description of your issue or question
  • Your device type and operating system (if applicable)
  • Any error messages you're seeing
  • Steps you've already tried to resolve the issue
