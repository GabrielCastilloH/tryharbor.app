Support

Last Updated: June 29, 2025

Need help with Harbor? We're here to support you on your journey to meaningful connections. Whether you have questions, technical issues, or just want to share feedback, we're listening.

Contact Us

For support, questions, or feedback, please contact us at:

  • tryharborapp@gmail.com

How We Can Support You

  • Technical issues that might be affecting your connections
  • Account setup and profile questions
  • Privacy and security concerns
  • Billing and subscription support
  • Feature suggestions to improve your experience
  • General questions about building meaningful connections

Response Time

We're committed to helping you succeed in building connections. We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days.

Before Contacting Us

To help us support you better, please include:

  • A clear description of your question or concern
  • Your device type and operating system (if applicable)
  • Any error messages you're seeing
  • Steps you've already tried to resolve the issue
  • Any specific context about your connection experience
